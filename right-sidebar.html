Step 4 : Communicating

Messaging

  • Sara (sara@example.com)

    • You: Hi Sara, I saw that you can teach music. I'm interested in learning how to play the guitar.
    • Sara: Hi, yes I can teach you guitar. Do you have any previous experience?
    • You: No, I'm a complete beginner. BTW im not a creep . Wanna meet?
    • Sara: How about next week?
    • Sara: Sounds good. Let's meet at the pines on Wednesday at 3pm.

  • John (john@example.com)

    • You: Hi John, I'm interested in learning how to cook. Can you teach me?
    • John: Sure, I'd be happy to teach you some cooking skills. What skills are you interested in learning?
    • You: I'd like to learn how to make pasta dishes.
    • John: Great, I can definitely teach you that. When would you like to start?
    • You: How about next weekend?
    • John: That works for me. Let's meet at my place at 10am on Saturday.

How this step works :

When the database matches you with someone, you get to text them. You can learn about the other person, be friends, learn things in person by setting up a meeting and what not.
