How this step works :

When you are done entering your skills and interests in step 2, the database then matches you with another user who has the skillset you are interested in and wants to learn something that you have in your skillset. You can also filter the search by selecting whether you want to learn from someone who is on campus or not. That way you are only making connections with people from a specific area.
